Fun Life Rafting

A Niger river rafting trip is one of the most spectacular ways to enjoy the natural beauty of the different vegetations of Nigeria. We offer whitewater rafting, inflatable kayak, and stand up paddleboard trips on multiple rivers in Nigeria. Our Fun Life rafting trips depart daily during the summer months from our basecamps in Abraka, Obaghoro, Okene and Markodi.
